Hu Shi’s letter to his son:

I nurture you, not a kindness, but a biological instinct caused by blood; therefore, since I am unkind to you, you do not need to repay me.

Instead, I want to thank you because my life is more complete because of your participation.

I just happened to be your father, you just happened to be my daughter and my son, I’m not your prequel, and you’re not my sequel.

You are a separate entity, a different soul from me; you did not come because of me, you came because of a desire for life.

You are free and I love you; but I will never control your life “in the name of love”.
