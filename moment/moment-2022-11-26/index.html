Keep faith. On the other side of your pain, is something good.
cn en

2022-11-26 1 min read moment Aeron

I got thanatophobia.

Started in 2018, when I was just 16.

It stayed with me through the years.

And continuing for the rest of my life, until I died.

However, I will not give up fighting it, and I will continue to do so for the rest of my life.

We are born with nothing, and we leave alone.

In this short life, the most precious thing is the journey of life itself.

If your family is healthy and has more than enough to live,

Then please enjoy life, feel every moment of surprise, every moment of joy.

I hope you can also cherish this hard-won journey, the only one in your life, that belongs to you alone.

See you, travelers.
